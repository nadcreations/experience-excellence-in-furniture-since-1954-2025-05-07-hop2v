About Us

Founded in 1954, Al Mutlaq Furniture began as a small family-owned workshop dedicated to crafting quality furniture. Through decades of commitment to excellence and innovation, we've grown into one of the region's leading furniture manufacturers and suppliers. Our journey has been marked by continuous improvement, adaptation to changing market needs, and an unwavering focus on quality.

Our mission is to transform spaces through exceptional furniture solutions that combine functionality, aesthetics, and durability. We believe in sustainable practices, innovative design, and creating lasting relationships with our clients. Our core values of integrity, excellence, and customer satisfaction guide every project we undertake.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What is your design and consultation process?

Our process begins with a detailed consultation to understand your needs and vision. We then create custom designs, provide 3D visualizations, and work closely with you to refine the solution until it perfectly matches your requirements. Our team handles everything from concept to installation.

What types of warranty and after-sales support do you offer?

We provide comprehensive warranty coverage ranging from 5-10 years depending on the product line. Our after-sales support includes maintenance services, repairs, and replacement parts. We're committed to ensuring your long-term satisfaction with our products.

How do you ensure quality in your furniture?

Quality control is integral to our process. We use premium materials, employ skilled craftsmen, and conduct rigorous testing at every production stage. Each piece must pass multiple quality checks before delivery, ensuring durability and perfection in every detail.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small projects typically take 4-6 weeks, while larger office fitouts may take 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and keep you updated throughout the process.

Do you offer customization options?

Yes, we specialize in custom furniture solutions. From materials and finishes to dimensions and functionality, we can tailor every aspect to meet your specific requirements. Our design team works closely with you to create unique pieces that perfectly fit your space and style.
